Microwave Peanut Butter & Banana Mug Cake

This gluten-free mug cake packed with protein that's made with only all-nautral ingredients gives you the perfect energy boost throughout the day especially after a work out. It's ideal as you can mix it up the night before and have it microwaved fresh in the morning or whenever you feeling peckish...

  • Prep Time - 5 mins
  • Cook Time - 1 min
  • Total Time - 6 mins
  • Serving - 1
  • Calories - 193

Ingredients

  • ½ medium banana (mashed)
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1 egg white
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 ½ tablespoons (30g) oat flour
  • ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a large microwavable mug mash the banana with a fork
  2. Mix in the peanut butter, egg whites and vanilla
  3. Add in the flour, cinnamon, baking powder and salt and gently stir in until combined
  4. Microwave for 45 second- 1 minute or until it is firm in the middle (timing is based on my 1200W microwave so your timing might vary)
  5. Top with some more sliced banana and enjoy warm

1 Minute Microwave Mug Brownie

An indulgent vegan chocolate brownie whipped up in just 1 minute in the microwave and will with 100% confidence satisfy your greatest chocolate craving

  • Prep Time - 5 mins
  • Cook Time - 1 min
  • Total Time - 6 mins
  • Serving - 1
  • Calories - 492

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons all purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder (100% cocoa)
  • 3 tablespoons flavorless oil (canola oil/coconut oil)
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons dairy-free chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a microwavable mug (roughly 1 cup measurement) mix together all of the ingredients
  2. Mix in the chocolate chips
  3. If you don’t want to cook them off straight away, then place them in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours
  4. Microwave for 45-60 seconds. (Timing is based on my 1200W microwave so your timing might vary). Allow to cool for at least 5 minutes
  5. Serve warm with some ice cream & enjoy!

Eggless Blueberry Microwave Mug Cake

A quick eggless blueberry mug cake that can be ready to eat in under 2 minutes in a microwave. With no eggs or oven require, this a must-try low-calorie recipe for those with a sweet craving

  • Prep Time - 1 mins
  • Cook Time - 2 min
  • Total Time - 3 mins
  • Serving - 2
  • Calories - 268 per serving

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter (melted)
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 7 – 8 Blueberries

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together butter, sugar, milk and vanilla extract
  2. Add baking powder and flour and whisk till combined
  3. Mix in blueberries and pour into 2 microwave proof tea cups or one mug - Only fill the cups or mugs halfway because the cake rises up quite a bit
  4. Microwave for 1 minute on high
  5. Serve the blueberry mug cake dusted with sugar and topped with more blueberries
