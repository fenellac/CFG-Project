Microwave Peanut Butter & Banana Mug Cake

This gluten-free mug cake packed with protein that's made with only all-nautral ingredients gives you the perfect energy boost throughout the day especially after a work out. It's ideal as you can mix it up the night before and have it microwaved fresh in the morning or whenever you feeling peckish...

  • Prep Time - 5 mins
  • Cook Time - 1 min
  • Total Time - 6 mins
  • Serving - 1
  • Calories - 193

Ingredients

  • ½ medium banana (mashed)
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1 egg white
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 ½ tablespoons (30g) oat flour
  • ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a large microwavable mug mash the banana with a fork
  2. Mix in the peanut butter, egg whites and vanilla
  3. Add in the flour, cinnamon, baking powder and salt and gently stir in until combined
  4. Microwave for 45 second- 1 minute or until it is firm in the middle (timing is based on my 1200W microwave so your timing might vary)
  5. Top with some more sliced banana and enjoy warm
